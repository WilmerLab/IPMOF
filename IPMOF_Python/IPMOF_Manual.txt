----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- read_ff_parameters(excel_file_path, ff_selection)
    ff_selection = 'uff' or 'dre'
    excel_file_path = '/home/kutay/Documents/Research/FF_Parameters.xlsx'
Ex:
uff = read_ff_parameters(excel_file_path, 'uff')

Out:
uff = {'atom': [*atom names*], 'sigma': [*atom sigmas*], 'epsilon': [*atom eps*]}
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- get_ff_parameters(atom_names, ff_parameters)
    atom_names = ['Cu', 'H', 'O']
    ff_parameters = uff (should be obtained from read_ff_parameters)

Ex:
atom_list_ff = get_ff_parameters(atom_names, uff)

Out:
atom_list_ff = [['Cu', 3.113691019900486, 2.5177788478012264],
                ['H', 2.5711337005530193, 22.156453860650792],
                ['O', 3.1181455134911875, 30.213346173614717]]
Notes:
* sigma values in Angstrom
* epsilon values in kB / K
